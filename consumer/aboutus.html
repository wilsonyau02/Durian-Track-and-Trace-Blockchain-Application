The Durian Track and Trace Blockchain Application (DTTBA) is a revolutionary application tool that is developed to allow users to trace the durian from farmer to end consumer in a secure and transparent way. This blockchain application aims to address the issue of the traceability in the durian industry, such as distribution of the durian, receiving of durian from the retailers, tracing of durian provenance by the consumers as well as the lack of transparency and accountability in the durian supply chain.

Objectives of the DTTBA are to create traceability, transparency, and accountability for each of the durians and to obtain and record feedback information on durians purchased from the end consumers. The information may include the durian farm and tree from where it was harvested, the date of harvest, and the date of the durian received by the retailers.
